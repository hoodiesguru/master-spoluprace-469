Location Strategies for Global Networks

Publikoval Jan Černý | 17. 07. 2026 | ⏰ 1 min čtení

Regional relevance is crucial. When deploying a network of sites, attaching precise geographic metadata (such as ICBM coordinates and Dublin Core schemas) assists crawlers understand the resource.

By rotating local business schemas across sites, entities can simulate natural geographic distribution without running physical infrastructure in those areas.
